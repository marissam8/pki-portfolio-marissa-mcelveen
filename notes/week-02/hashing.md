## Part 3 — Observations
Document the following in your Week 2 notes:

- The hashing changed because a single character was added the the file. Hashing accounts for every character, number, or space in a document.
- Hashing is detecting alteration of data, not the encryption of it. 
- The security property hashing provides is integrity.
- Hashing is used in PKI systems such as digital certificates. One component of verifying a digital certificate is checking its signature. Hashing helps verify certificate integrity by ensuring that none of the certificate data has been altered since it was signed.
